NYC DOB Department Tool Kits

As of 2 January, 2016, the New York City Department of Buildings' "Tool Kits" page is full of dead links. After locating one of these tool kit PDFs via google search, I was able to figure out the correct URL for all of them, and have provided them here.

In 2011, the Department launched a new brochure series called the "Tool Kit." The Tool Kits allow individuals to pick up all Department brochures in one folder. There are two types of Tool Kits, one geared toward homeowners and the other geared towards industry professionals.
